Privacy Policy for Color My Mood Extension

Introduction

We at Color My Mood take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines the types of information we collect and how it's used and protected.

Information Collection and Use

Color My Mood is a non-intrusive extension that does not collect or store any personal data from its users. The extension operates entirely locally on your device, and no information is sent to external servers or third parties. User-Selected Mood Data: Color My Mood allows you to select a mood to change the color theme of websites you visit. This data is not recorded or stored and is only used temporarily to apply the selected theme. Browsing Data: We do not track, collect, or store your browsing history, website content, or any other data related to your online activities.

Data Protection

Since Color My Mood does not collect or store personal data, there are no risks associated with data breaches or misuse of personal information. All interactions with the extension are confined to your local device.

User Consent

By installing and using Color My Mood, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. No further consent is required as we do not collect or store personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page and, where appropriate, notified to you by other means. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
